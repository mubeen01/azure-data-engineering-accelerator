# Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and professional.
- Welcome constructive feedback.
- Collaborate in good faith.
- Focus on solving technical problems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Offensive language
- Spam or disruptive behavior

Project maintainers may remove contributions or restrict participation if these guidelines are repeatedly violated.

Thank you for helping build a positive open-source community.
